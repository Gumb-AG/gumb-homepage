What are our core values?

The basis of all our decisions

User-oriented.

Open.

Continuously improve.

Global & local.

We focus on the user

Everything we do must start and end with our users in mind. We advocate for you, your club, your association and team, and we aim to exceed your expectations with everything we do.

We're always open

With our users and with each other. We’re open to new ideas, new ways of working and open to being challenged.

We continuously improve

We don’t stand still. We continuously improve everything; the product, the experience, the support. We love feedback from users & groups of any kind.

We think globally, we act locally
